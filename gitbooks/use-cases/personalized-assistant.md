# Personalized Assistant

{% hint style="warning" %}
This use case is a **work in progress**. We're actively building and refining it with early partners.
{% endhint %}

Most AI assistants start every conversation from zero. A TinyCortex-powered personal assistant remembers your preferences, habits, past requests, and context so it gets better the more you use it.

## How It Works

* **Learns your preferences**: The assistant remembers how you like things done your communication style, preferred tools, dietary restrictions, scheduling habits without you having to repeat yourself.
* **Accumulates context over time**: Past conversations, decisions, and outcomes are all stored. When you ask "what did I decide about the vendor contract?", it knows.
* **Forgets what doesn't matter**: Casual one-off requests fade naturally. The important things your recurring commitments, key relationships, long-running projects stay sharp.
* **Cross-session continuity**: Pick up exactly where you left off. The assistant connects context across days, weeks, and months of interaction.

## Example

You tell your assistant in January that you're vegetarian. In March, when you ask it to suggest restaurants for a team dinner, it filters for vegetarian-friendly options automatically without a reminder needed.
